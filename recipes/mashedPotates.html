Mashed Potatoes

The creamiest, smoothest mashed potatoes you will ever eat!

Ingredients

  • Yukon gold potatoes
  • Heavy cream
  • Milk
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Was the potatoes
  2. Fill a pot with cold water
  3. Put potatoes in pot with water and boil on high heat until tender
  4. In a seperate pot warm heavy cream, milk, and butter together until melted
  5. Drain potatoes
  6. Mash potatoes
  7. Combine mixture with potatoes
