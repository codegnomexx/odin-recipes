Pan Seared Steak

This is a great steak recipe to taste the meat and the char!

Ingredients

  • Rib eye steak
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Bring the steak up to room temperature
  2. Add oil to pan and heat pan to high
  3. Add salt and pepper to the steak
  4. Add steak to pan and rotate every 1.5 minutes 2 times
  5. Turn off the heat and add butter
  6. Baste the steak in butter
  7. Rest the steak for 5 minutes
  8. Cut into slices
